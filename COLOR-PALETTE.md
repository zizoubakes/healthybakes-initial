# Zizou's Healthy Bakes - Color Palette

## 🎨 Professional Organic Color Scheme

This palette was designed based on 2025 trends for organic bakeries, combining:
- **Earthy browns** for authenticity and warmth
- **Fresh sage green** for health and nature
- **Warm honey** for energy and comfort
- **Soft neutrals** for elegance and breathing room

---

## Color Breakdown

### Primary Colors

**Sage Green** - `#7BA05B`
- RGB: 123, 160, 91
- Use: Primary buttons, CTAs, brand accent
- Meaning: Fresh, healthy, natural, organic
- Where: Buttons, links, icons

**Terracotta** - `#D97757`
- RGB: 217, 119, 87
- Use: Secondary accent, highlights
- Meaning: Warm, friendly, energetic
- Where: Accent elements, category tags

**Olive Brown** - `#5D4E37`
- RGB: 93, 78, 55
- Use: Headers, important text
- Meaning: Grounded, authentic, natural
- Where: Main headlines, navigation

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text**: Olive Brown (#5D4E37) - Main content
- **Secondary Text**: Olive at 70% opacity - Supporting text
- **Light Text on Dark**: Soft White (#FFFDFB)

### Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: Sage Green (#7BA05B) with white text
- **Secondary**: Terracotta (#D97757) with white text
- **Outline**: Border in Sage with Sage text

### Backgrounds
- **Main**: Cream (#FAF7F2)
- **Sections**: Warm Beige (#EDE4D8)
- **Cards**: Soft White (#FFFDFB)
- **Footer**: Olive Brown (#5D4E37)

## Color Psychology

### Why This Palette Works for Healthy Bakery:

**🌿 Green (Sage)**
- Signals organic, natural, healthy
- Creates trust around health claims
- Appeals to eco-conscious moms

**🍂 Brown (Olive)**
- Conveys authenticity and tradition
- Suggests natural, unprocessed ingredients
- Creates warm, homey feeling

**🌅 Terracotta**
- Adds energy and warmth
- Stimulates appetite gently
- Friendly and approachable

**🍯 Honey Gold**
- Suggests natural sweetness
- Premium quality feeling
- Positive, optimistic vibe

**🥥 Cream/Beige**
- Creates spacious, breathable design
- Doesn't compete with food photos
- Elegant and clean

## Accessibility

All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards for contrast:

✅ Olive Brown on Cream - 8.2:1 (Excellent)
✅ Sage Green on Soft White - 4.6:1 (Good)
✅ Terracotta on Soft White - 4.1:1 (Good)
✅ Olive Brown on Warm Beige - 7.8:1 (Excellent)
