## 🎨 How to Create Your Logo (Easy Way)

### Step 1: Go to Canva (FREE)
1. Visit **canva.com**
2. Sign up free (use Google account)
3. Search for "bakery logo" templates

### Step 2: Choose a Style

**Option A: Simple Text Logo** (Easiest, 10 minutes)
- Find a template with just text
- Change text to: "Zizou's" (big) and "Healthy Bakes" (small)
- Use these fonts:
  - "Zizou's" → Playfair Display or Sacramento (elegant)
  - "Healthy Bakes" → Inter or Montserrat (clean)
- Colors: Use #A8C69F (sage green) and #E88D67 (terracotta)

**Option B: Logo with Icon** (20 minutes)
- Search templates with: leaf, wheat, heart, or rolling pin
- Replace text with "Zizou's Healthy Bakes"
- Keep it simple and clean
- Use your website colors

### Step 3: Download
1. Click "Share" → "Download"
2. Select "PNG" with transparent background
3. Save as `logo.png`

### Step 4: Add to Website
1. Save logo.png in `/public` folder
2. Tell me when done, I'll update the code to show it!
