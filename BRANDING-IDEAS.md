# Branding Ideas for Zineb/Zizou's Bakery

### Option 1: **Zizou's Healthy Bakes**
- Simple, personal, memorable
- "Zizou" is friendly and approachable
- Clear about what you do

- Professional with personal touch

- Easy to remember

### Option 4: **Zineb's Wholesome Treats**
- Professional and descriptive

- Modern, boutique feel

## Where to Get Logo Designed

### DIY Options (Free/Cheap):

1. **Canva** (canva.com) - FREE
   - Hundreds of templates
   - Easy drag-and-drop
   - Can export high-res for $12.99
   - BEST for non-designers

2. **Looka** (looka.com) - $20
   - AI-powered logo generator
   - Enter business name, get instant options
   - Includes brand kit (colors, fonts)
   - Very professional results

3. **Hatchful by Shopify** (hatchful.shopify.com) - FREE
   - Simple logo maker
   - Good templates
   - Free download

### Professional Options:

1. **Fiverr** ($25-100)
   - Real designers
   - Custom work
   - Get 3-5 concepts
   - Revisions included
   - Takes 3-5 days

2. **99designs** ($299+)
   - Design contest
   - Multiple designers compete
   - You pick the winner
   - Very high quality

3. **Local Designer** ($200-500)
   - Most personalized
   - Can work directly with you
   - Often supports local businesses

## Recommended Approach

**For Quick Launch:**
1. Use **Canva** to create a simple logo today (1 hour)
2. Use these elements:
   - Font: "Playfair Display" for "Zizou's"
   - Font: "Inter" for "Healthy Bakes"
   - Icon: Leaf, wheat, or heart
   - Colors: Your website colors (terracotta + sage)

**For Professional Brand:**
1. Start with Canva logo for now
2. Once business is established, hire a designer on Fiverr ($50-75)
3. Get logo variations:
   - Full logo (text + icon)
   - Icon only (for social media profile)
   - Text only (for headers)
   - Black & white version (for printing)

---

## Logo Specifications Needed

When you create/order a logo, get these files:

1. **PNG with transparent background** (for website)
   - Size: 500px wide minimum
   - For: Website header, social media

2. **SVG vector file** (scalable, never pixelates)
   - For: Professional printing
   - Can resize infinitely

3. **Variations:**
   - Horizontal layout (for website header)
   - Stacked layout (for business cards)
   - Icon only (for favicon, social profiles)

## Favicon (Website Icon)

Once you have a logo, create a favicon:
1. Use just the icon/initial from your logo
2. Make it 512x512px
3. Save as `favicon.ico` and `icon.png`
4. Place in `/public` folder
5. It will show in browser tabs!
